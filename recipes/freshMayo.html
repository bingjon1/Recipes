Fresh Mayonnaise

Description:

Simple, fresh and delicious mayonnaise recipe

Indgredients:

  • Vegetable oil
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Optional: Siracha

Steps:

  1. Grab immersion blender and set it up
  2. Pour 1 cup of oil into the immersion blender container
  3. Crack egg inside container
  4. Add juice of 1/2 of the lemon
  5. Add the 5-7 minced garlic cloves
  6. Add a tablespoon of dijon mustard
  7. Let everything settle to the bottom
  8. Insert immersion blender blade to the very bottom of the container
  9. Blend
  10. Once ingredients are combined, add salt to taste and blend for another 30 seconds
  11. Enjoy
