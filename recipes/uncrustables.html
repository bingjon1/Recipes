Incrustables Are Legendary

Description:

A sealed crustless sandwich is a foodstuff comprising a filling sealed between two layers of bread by a crimped edge, with the crust subsequently removed.

Indgredients:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

Steps:

  1. Take out 2 pieces of bread and lay them out on a plate
  2. Grab a butter knife and the jars of peanut butter and jelly
  3. Using the knife, spread peanut butter on 1 slice or bread and jelly on the other
  4. Combine the slices together and slice on a diagonal
  5. Enjoy.
