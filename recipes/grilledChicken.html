Grilled Chicken

Description:

Simple, juicy and delicious grilled chicken recipe that is quick to make

Indgredients:

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika

Steps:

  1. Place the raw chicken in a medium sized mixing bowl
  2. Add 2 spoonfuls of Mayo and the spices listed above
  3. Mix bowl until ingredients are thoroughly combined, set aside for 20-30 minutes
  4. Pre-heat skillet medium-high heat
  5. Place each piece flat on the hot skillet
  6. Cook until chicken is 165 degrees farenheit internally
  7. Enjoy.
